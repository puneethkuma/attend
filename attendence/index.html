Core Features

Everything you need to manage your workforce

From real-time clock-ins to AI-powered analytics — Attendance24 simplifies every aspect of HR and attendance management so your team can focus on what matters.

🕐
Real-Time Attendance Tracking

Employees clock in and out from mobile, web, or biometric devices. Your dashboard updates instantly with accurate timestamps and location data — zero manual entry required.

📍
GPS Location Verification

Geo-fencing technology ensures employees can only check in from approved work locations. Essential for field staff, remote workers, and multi-site operations.

🗓️
Shift & Schedule Management

Build flexible shift patterns, rotating schedules, and split duties with ease. Automatically notify employees of upcoming shifts via SMS or app push notification.

✈️
Leave Management

Employees submit leave requests in seconds. Managers approve instantly. Leave balances, accruals, and calendar integration are handled automatically — no paperwork needed.

📊
Reports & Analytics

Generate detailed attendance reports, overtime summaries, and department-wise insights on demand. Schedule automated delivery and export to PDF or Excel in one click.

💰
Payroll Integration

Automatically calculate wages, overtime pay, and deductions based on attendance data. Seamlessly sync with Razorpay Payroll, Zoho, and other major payroll providers.

🤖
AI-Powered Insights & Alerts

Predictive absenteeism alerts flag attendance patterns before they impact your team. Machine learning detects anomalies and gives managers actionable, data-backed recommendations.

☁️
Cloud Dashboard

Access your complete workforce picture from any device, anywhere in the world. Role-based permissions ensure every team member sees exactly what they need — nothing more.

🔗
50+ App Integrations

Connect seamlessly with Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, SAP, Workday, BambooHR, and more via our open REST API and Zapier integration — no coding needed.
